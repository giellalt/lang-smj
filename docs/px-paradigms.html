Background

This document is here to enlighten a murky part of the Lule Sámi grammar, namely its possessive suffixes. The normative grammars are not precise enough, and the following presentation is from Grundström's dictionary and Lehtirantas work on the sámi in Arjeplog. What we find in the dialects are two parallel systems, we have tried to be inclusive and make reference to both systems here. They are not yet included in the analyser, but an implementation is forthcoming.
