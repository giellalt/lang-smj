REGULAR EXPRESSIONS
.=match any character
?=match zero or one character
*=match zero or more characters
+=match one or more characters
{n}=match n times
{m,n}=match m through n times
{n,}=match n or more times
^=beginning of a line
$=end of a line
[abc]=match one of enclosed characters
[^abc]=exclude enclosed characters
[:class:]=match a character class, i.e.:
[:alpha:] for letters
[:space:] for white space
[:punct:] for punctuation
[:upper:] for upper-case letters
[[:<:]]=match beginning of words
[[:>:]]=match ending of words
|used to separate alternatives
